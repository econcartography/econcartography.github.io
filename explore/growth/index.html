Explore. Undestand. Grow.

Why are we caring about International Trade (and we think you should do the same)? According to (some influential source here, with link) the share of International Trade on our global economy is growing at a fast pace year by year. With this in mind, our purpose it to provide both individuals and country-level policy makers the tools to identify and unleash the growth potential hidden in real life complexity. With this service, individual users can identify for each product the most profitable investment conditions. Policy makers can find the lists of products that have the potential to enhance the economy of a whole country.

To do so, we offer an extensive exploration of the export patterns of EU Members. Our dashboard shows the yearly patterns of exported products for all EU Members between 1999 and 2017. Users are free to navigate products - the black dots -, countries - the coloured dots - and the corresponding export patterns. The colors of countries reflect clusters of Member States with similar export activity. Clicking on a node or a link reveals interactive plots of properties taken from different data sources, showing a complete landscapes of European International Trade. Each link has two attributes: “global export” (check the name), i.e. the value in USD of the exported product, and “rca”, which is a measure of trading competitiveness. The nodes comes with aggregated information that relate the state of their global economy with their trading performance.

Individual users

When setting up a new investment even the tiniest detail can make the difference.

  • What is the most profitable context for a new export activity?
  • How did my sector perform in the previous years at global/national scale?
  • What is the level of economic complexity of my product?

All this answers find a question in our dashboard. From the Filter Panel you can find the countries that are more competitive in the export of a given product (more information on the rca here). From the Link/Node Panels you can observe the evolution of global/national value of export for single products. Clicking on a product you can monitor its complexity over time (complexity is a measure of its diversification across countries).

Policy makers

Trading competitiveness is strongly related with many macroeconomic indicator of national wealth. For this reason, optimizing export patterns is crucial for the overall wellness of a country. Looking at coloured nodes you can explore their competitiveness in time. As competitiveness grows when a country invests on products with high complexity, our dashboard allows policy makers to easily pick the products that are most effective in increasing their national trading performance. Moreover, identifying the other Member States with similar or diverging export patterns is a quick way to identify potentially fruitfull international collaborations.
