About

Are you an entrepreneur willing to start a new export activity, a policy maker interested in increasing the competitiveness of your country or just a citizen curious about the cohesion of European Union? No matter who you are, finding your way along the complex, tiny details of European economies can be a hard task. Our dashboards offer intuitive tools to answer complex questions, helping you find the most profitable investment opportunities or simply satisfy your curiosity.

Extract

Putting together Eurostat, Comtrade and SWIFT we offer you the possibility of exploring a massive dataset rich of details on national macroeconomic indicators, import/export trade and payment flows between Member States.

Analyse

Thanks to our background as Researchers and Data Scientists we can exploit the most recent literature on economic complexity to answer your questions.

Deliver

With FNAlab, we can show you the results of our analysis in the most efficient way possible. Forget exploding tables and infinite texts. Think networks.
